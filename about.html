About The
Artist

The artist, known as Freddie Freeloader, has one wish; to express completely what he believes to be the current zeitgeist.

Biography

Through lengthy discourses of nihilism and a sliver of pure, unadulterated and child-like hope, he conveys his music to the ones eager and willing to open their arms — or in this case, ears — and embrace a hug capable of crushing their suspended ribs.

The music he states "is in its embryonic state": brash, immature, whining, repetitive and full of the charisma a wunderkind could bring you.

The artist extends his hand, and in his endless illness, as we traverse through the garden of humanity — wilted, tumultuous, downtrodden and diverse — we adjust our eyes slightly, to meet our saviour: a comforting death, with as little suffering dealt and caused.

This life yearns beauty, and only beauty. Here, I can feel it.
