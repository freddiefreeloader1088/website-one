About This Record Read

Mouth of a Cave is a singer-songwriter, experimental hip-hop, art-pop, drone-folk, tribal ambient record. Recorded between May 31st and October 6th, 2025, it is a time capsule of the artist's mental state and his amateurish, unguarded world-view.

Taking André 3000's words — "from little rooms come great things" — to heart, Mouth of a Cave is an hour-long musical experience recorded front to back within the bedroom of the artist's childhood home. There is no other presence on this record.

With words aimed at inciting feelings of revolution, sentences that contort the listener's perspective, and an approach to genre and production striving for an idiosyncratic identity, every inch of this album is given careful deliberation and intent — all while reaching for something experimental, raw, and its own.
